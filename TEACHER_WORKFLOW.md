# Premium Multi-Branch Academic Operations Platform

---

# Purpose

Dokumen ini mendefinisikan:

* teacher operational workflow,
* teacher experience philosophy,
* teaching dashboard structure,
* attendance workflow,
* lesson reporting workflow,
* dan anti-burnout operational design.

Teacher adalah:

* frontline operational user,
* academic delivery engine,
* dan emotional interaction point terbesar antara academy dan student.

Platform harus membantu teacher:

* bekerja lebih teratur,
* lebih ringan,
* lebih cepat,
* dan lebih stabil secara operasional.

---

# Core Teacher Workflow Philosophy

---

# 1. Teacher Energy Preservation

Teacher bukan:

* operator data,
* admin spreadsheet,
* atau mesin input form.

Teacher adalah:

* pengajar,
* mentor,
* dan academic performer.

---

# Rule

Platform harus:

* mengurangi friction,
* mengurangi repetitive task,
* mengurangi confusion,
* dan mengurangi operational stress.

---

# Forbidden Experience

Teacher tidak boleh merasa:

* dashboard terlalu ramai,
* terlalu banyak klik,
* terlalu banyak popup,
* atau workflow terlalu berat.

---

# 2. Daily Operational Simplicity

Teacher berpikir berdasarkan:

* waktu,
* kelas berikutnya,
* student,
* dan materi.

Bukan:

* menu bertingkat,
* tabel kompleks,
* atau operational analytics.

---

# Rule

Teacher dashboard harus:

* focused,
* chronological,
* operationally fast,
* dan mobile-friendly.

---

# 3. Teaching Stability Is Critical

Jika teacher workflow kacau:

* attendance terlambat,
* lesson summary kosong,
* parent trust turun,
* dan branch operational quality menurun.

---

# Rule

Semua teacher workflow wajib:

* realtime,
* stable,
* dan low-friction.
